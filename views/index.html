Hospital-Grade Surface Disinfectant Selector

Welcome to the Selector!

This tool is designed to help medical professionals choose the best surface disinfectant product for their specific needs. Infection control and prevention nurses, environmental services managers, and anyone involved in keeping a medical facility free of disease-causing pathogens, are invited to use this tool.

How to use this tool:

This selector is very easy to use! If you are not familiar with medical facility surface disinfection, please take a moment to review the slides at right.

Just click on the Continue button, and answer the four questions in the questionnaire. Note that products with both soft and hard surface claims will only be listed if that option is selected, since these products are generally more expensive. Know the Spaulding level of kill you need for your situation, and select the appropriate option in question 3. Because product cost is specific to your facility and purchasing organization, cost is not addressed in this tool, but a cost calculator spreadsheet is available for download at the bottom of the results page.

We hope this tool is useful for you in preventing the spread of disease in your facility.

For more information about surface disinfection, and infection control and prevention, please visit APIC's (the Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology) Education page
